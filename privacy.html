Privacy

seiche.info · last updated 9 July 2026 · DRAFT pending review

The short version

This site collects almost nothing. There are no ads, no trackers, no analytics scripts, and nothing is sold to anyone.

What is collected

Visitors: standard server and network logs (IP address, user agent, requested pages) held briefly by our hosting and network providers (GitHub Pages, Cloudflare, Hetzner) for security and operations. We run no analytics of our own.

Subscribers: a username you choose and a salted hash of your password. We never store the password itself. A signed session token is kept in your browser's local storage for up to 30 days; signing out removes it. If you email us, we keep the correspondence.

What is not collected

No real name is required for an account. No payment details are stored on our servers; when paid plans launch, payments will be handled by a payment processor and we will see only what the processor confirms, never your card or bank details.

Where it lives

Account data lives on a server in Germany (Hetzner). The public site is served by GitHub Pages behind Cloudflare. Email is handled by Titan via GoDaddy.

Your choices

Ask desk@seiche.info to see or delete what we hold about you and it will be done within a reasonable time. Deleting your account removes your username and password hash.
